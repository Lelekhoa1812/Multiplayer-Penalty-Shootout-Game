Penalty Shootout Game

×

This is the Penalty shootout game, you will be either a shooter or a goalie to play against your friend. You have 10 seconds to make your decision out-minding your friend. If you are a shooter, drag the ball to score from your left, right side or to the middle, and if you are a goalkeeper, drag the glove to deny the goal from your left, right side or to the middle.

Please wait until your opponent enters the room.
